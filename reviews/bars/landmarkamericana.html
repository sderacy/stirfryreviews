Glassboro's Gem

I recently had the pleasure of visiting the Landmark Americana in Glassboro, New Jersey and I must say, I was very impressed. The restaurant has a welcoming and lively atmosphere, and the staff were friendly and attentive.

One thing that stood out to me was the menu - it offered a wide variety of options, including classic bar food, sandwiches, salads, and more. I opted for the Landmark Burger, which was cooked to perfection and topped with bacon and cheese. The fries were crispy and delicious, and the serving size was generous.

In addition to the burger, I also tried the buffalo chicken dip, which was a standout appetizer. The dip was creamy and had just the right amount of spice, and the tortilla chips that came with it were crispy and fresh.

The Landmark Americana also has a great selection of beers on tap, and the bartenders were knowledgeable and helpful in making recommendations. They also have a happy hour from 3-6pm on weekdays with discounted drinks and appetizers.

Overall, I would highly recommend the Landmark Americana in Glassboro to anyone looking for a great bar and restaurant with delicious food and a lively atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a casual night out with friends or a place to catch the game, this spot has something for everyone.

- Stirfry
