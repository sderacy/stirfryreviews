Unforgettable Haitian Cuisine

I recently had the pleasure of dining at Zanmi, and I must say, it was an unforgettable experience. From the moment I stepped foot in the restaurant, I was greeted with warm and friendly smiles from the staff, who went out of their way to ensure my dining experience was exceptional.

As a food lover, I was blown away by the menu at Zanmi, which offered a unique fusion of Haitian and Caribbean cuisine. I started off with the griot, which is a Haitian-style fried pork dish, and it was absolutely delicious. The meat was tender and well-seasoned, and the accompanying pikliz, a spicy slaw made with cabbage and vinegar, added the perfect amount of tanginess to the dish.

For my main course, I tried the voodoo pasta, which was slow-cooked to perfection and packed with flavor. The pasta was cooked perfectly and the garnishments blended very well with the rest of the dish.

To finish off the meal, I indulged in the Haitian-style bread pudding, which was a delightful mix of bread, raisins, and spices, topped with a creamy caramel sauce. It was the perfect ending to an amazing meal.

Overall, I highly recommend Zanmi to anyone looking for an unforgettable dining experience. The food is delicious, the staff is friendly and welcoming, and the atmosphere is cozy and inviting. I can't wait to go back and try more of their amazing dishes!

- Stirfry
