Soul of Harlem

I recently had the pleasure of dining at Melba's Restaurant, and I must say, it was an incredible experience. From the moment I stepped into the restaurant, I was greeted with a warm and inviting atmosphere, complete with live music and friendly staff.

Melba's menu is inspired by Southern and soul food cuisine, and it did not disappoint. I started off with the fried chicken and waffles, which was the perfect combination of sweet and savory. The chicken was perfectly seasoned and had a crispy coating, while the waffles were fluffy and delicious.

For my main course, I tried the shrimp and grits, which was a standout dish. The shrimp was perfectly cooked and had a nice kick of spice, while the grits were creamy and flavorful. The dish was topped with a rich and savory gravy that brought all the flavors together.

To finish off the meal, I indulged in the sweet potato pie, which was the perfect ending to a delicious meal. The pie was perfectly spiced and had a flaky, buttery crust.

Overall, I would highly recommend Melba's Restaurant to anyone looking for an unforgettable dining experience. The food is delicious, the atmosphere is inviting, and the staff is friendly and welcoming. It's the perfect spot for a casual night out with friends or a special occasion.

P.S. Try the mac and cheese! That was pretty good too.

- Stirfry
