Wholesome Pizza Joint in Ewing

If you're looking for a great pizza place in Ewing, New Jersey, look no further than Ruben's Pizza. This unassuming pizzeria may not look like much from the outside, but once you take a bite of their pizza, you'll understand why it's a local favorite.

The crust at Ruben's Pizza is crispy and has a great texture that's not too thin or too thick. The sauce is tangy and flavorful, and the cheese is perfectly melted and gooey. They also offer a wide range of toppings, from classic pepperoni to more unique options like roasted red peppers and artichokes.

But Ruben's Pizza isn't just known for their great pizza - they also have a menu of delicious Italian dishes that are definitely worth trying. Their calzones are especially popular, and the stromboli is a great choice if you're in the mood for something a little different. And if you're looking for a lighter option, their salads are fresh and flavorful.

The staff at Ruben's Pizza is always friendly and welcoming, and the prices are very reasonable. The restaurant has a laid-back, casual atmosphere that's perfect for families or groups of friends.

Overall, I highly recommend Ruben's Pizza for anyone in the Ewing area who loves pizza and Italian cuisine. It's a great spot for a casual dinner or a quick lunch, and the food is always delicious.

- Stirfry
