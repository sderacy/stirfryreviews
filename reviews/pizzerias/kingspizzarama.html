Familial Pizza Joint in Ewing

If you're looking for delicious pizza and Italian food in Ewing, New Jersey, then King's Pizzarama is definitely worth a visit. This cozy pizzeria has been a local favorite for years, and it's easy to see why.

The pizza at King's Pizzarama is some of the best I've ever had. The crust is perfectly crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside, and the sauce is flavorful without being too overpowering. The cheese is gooey and delicious, and they offer a wide range of toppings, from classic pepperoni to more unique options like eggplant and spinach.

But King's Pizzarama isn't just about pizza. Their menu also includes a variety of other Italian dishes, like pasta, sandwiches, and salads. The vodka rigatoni is particularly delicious, with rich, creamy, and tasty sauce.

The staff at King's Pizzarama is always friendly and welcoming, and they're happy to make recommendations if you're not sure what to order. The restaurant has a cozy, old-school vibe that's perfect for a casual dinner with friends or family.

And the prices at King's Pizzarama are very reasonable, especially considering the quality of the food. You can easily feed a family without breaking the bank.

Overall, I highly recommend King's Pizzarama for anyone in the Ewing area who loves pizza and Italian cuisine. It's a local gem that's definitely worth a visit.

- Stirfry
