Pizzerias / Mamma Flora's Trattoria

1750 N Olden Ave, Ewing Township, NJ 08638 • mammaflorastrattoria.com

May 15, 2023

Mother of All Ewing Pizzerias

If you're looking for authentic Italian-style pizza, Mamma Flora's Trattoria is the place to go. This cozy pizzeria is a hidden gem that's definitely worth seeking out. The moment you step inside, you're transported to Italy with its rustic decor and warm, welcoming atmosphere.

The star of the show, of course, is the pizza. The crust is crispy on the outside and soft and chewy on the inside, just as it should be. The sauce is made from fresh tomatoes and has the perfect balance of sweetness and acidity. And the toppings are all high-quality and flavorful. I highly recommend trying the Margherita pizza, which is a classic for a reason.

But the pizza isn't the only thing that's delicious at Mamma Flora's Trattoria. Their appetizers, like the bruschetta and the arancini, are fantastic, and their pasta dishes are also worth trying. I particularly enjoyed the veal parm, which was perfectly cooked and had a rich, hearty sauce.

The service at Mamma Flora's Trattoria is also top-notch. The staff is friendly and attentive, and they're happy to make recommendations if you're not sure what to order. And the prices are very reasonable, especially considering the high quality of the food.

Overall, I highly recommend Mamma Flora's Trattoria for anyone who loves pizza and Italian cuisine. It's a wonderful place to relax with friends or family and enjoy some delicious food in a cozy atmosphere.

- Stirfry
