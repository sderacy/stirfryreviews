Best Artichoke Pizza in NYC

As a lover of New York-style pizza, I was eager to try Artichoke Basille's Pizza, a popular pizzeria located in the heart of New York City. Upon entering the restaurant, I was immediately struck by the inviting and laid-back atmosphere, with colorful walls adorned with vintage pizza boxes and photographs.

I ordered the classic Margherita pizza and was blown away by its simple yet flavorful combination of tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. The crust was crispy and had a perfect chewiness, and the cheese melted to perfection.

Another pizza I tried was the Crab pizza, which had a unique and decadent combination of creamy Alfredo sauce, fresh crab meat, mozzarella cheese, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes. The crab meat was tender and sweet, and the Alfredo sauce was rich and creamy without being too heavy.

Aside from the pizza, I also enjoyed the Fried Calamari appetizer, which was crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. It was served with a tangy and spicy marinara sauce that perfectly complemented the mild flavor of the calamari.

The service at Artichoke Basille's Pizza was friendly and efficient, and the atmosphere was lively and welcoming. The prices were reasonable for the quality of the food, and the portions were generous.

I tried the Artichoke Basille's Signature artichoke pizza and it was almost good enough to override my hate of Artichoke. I'll let you decide how good that is!

Overall, I would highly recommend Artichoke Basille's Pizza to any pizza lover looking for a delicious and authentic New York-style pie in the heart of the city. The quality of the ingredients and the attention to detail in the preparation make for a truly exceptional dining experience.

- Stirfry
