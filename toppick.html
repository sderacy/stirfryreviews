Top Pick Review:
Ajika Ramen

1400 Parkway Ave, Ewing Township, NJ 08628 • Ajika's Menusite

The Scorecard
Service ★★★★★
Food ★★★★★
Color Combination ★★★★★
Visual Appeal ★★★★★
Freshness ★★★★★

May 15, 2023

Appetizing Asian Fusion Cuisine

As someone who loves ramen, I have tried many different ramen spots in my area, but Ajika Ramen has quickly become my favorite place to go for a delicious and satisfying bowl of noodles. First of all, the broth at Ajika Ramen is absolutely outstanding. It is rich, flavorful, and has just the right amount of depth and complexity to keep you coming back for more. The tonkotsu ramen, in particular,is one of my favorites with its creamy and porky broth that's been simmered for hours.

The noodles themselves are also top-notch. They are cooked perfectly al dente and have a great chewy texture that holds up well in the broth. I also love that they offer several different types of noodles to choose from, including thick and thin varieties. But what really sets Ajika Ramen apart for me is their attention to detail and the quality of their ingredients. The chashu (braised pork belly) is melt-in-your-mouth tender and the soft-boiled eggs are perfectly cooked with a just-set yolk. The toppings are always fresh and plentiful, including crunchy beansprouts, scallions, and nori.

In addition to their fantastic ramen, Ajika Ramen also offers a variety of delicious appetizers andsides, like the gyoza dumplings and crispy chicken karaage. The atmosphere at Ajika Ramen is cozy and welcoming, with friendly service and a relaxed vibe. And even though it can get crowded during peak hours, the wait is always worth it.

Overall, I would highly recommend Ajika Ramen to anyone looking for a delicious and satisfying bowl of noodles. It has quickly become my favorite ramen spot and I can't wait to go back again and again.

- Stirfry
